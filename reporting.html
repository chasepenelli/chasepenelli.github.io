Time Well Spent

Any business should understand what is most important, how to track it, and what actions need to be taken to succeed. Underrepresented / Inclustion Efforts

Chicago is a melting pot which helps to drive the mission and spread the word on how coding is for everyone. It doesn't matter ones background. In todays world, anything is possible if you put your mind to it and that is the message I want to continue to spread to let anyone know that a STEM career is obtainable and fufilling. Diversity brings huge oppertunites, especially in Chicago, to strengthing Flatiron Schools position in this space. With such a rich cultural makeup, tapping into these markets is high priority. Identifiying and engaging with these groups has the ability to create a steady stream of qualified applicants. Paying attention to who converts the most, where there a growing need, and how best to address it will shape the success of the Chicago launch.

Community Outreach

As far as the mid-west is concerned, we are the biggest tech hub which is something we will be tapping into. Chicago boasts some of the largest tech communities outside of San Francisco and New York. In addition, there is a thriving community college market that can be leveraged. The focus will be on how leverage and represent Flatiron School within these communities.
